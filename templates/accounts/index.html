Process

How ResumeAI works

Three steps from job post to shortlist — fully automated, no manual screening.

{# Candidate flow #}

For Candidates

1

Browse & Apply

Find open positions that match your skills. Upload your resume — PDF only, text-based for best results.

2

Get Scored Instantly

Our NLP engine compares your resume against the job description, required skills, experience, and qualifications.

3

Track Your Result

See your rank and score in the candidate list once the deadline passes. Know exactly where you stand.

{# HR flow #}

For HR / Recruiters

1

Post a Job

Create a listing with description, required skills, experience level, qualifications, and a deadline.

2

Resumes Auto-Screened

Every application is scored automatically. No manual reading — the system ranks all candidates by match quality.

3

Shortlist & Interview

Review the ranked list after the deadline. Approve or reject — top scorers are pre-highlighted for you.

{# ══════════════ FEATURES ══════════════ #}

Features

Built for precision hiring

Every feature is designed around one goal — the right candidate in front of the right employer, faster.

Weighted Scoring Algorithm

Skills (35%), experience (25%), qualifications (20%), and content relevance via cosine similarity (20%) — transparent and explainable.

PDF Resume Extraction

Text is extracted directly from uploaded PDFs — skills, experience, and qualifications parsed automatically using NLP.

Ranked Candidate List

All applicants ranked by score after the deadline. HR sees a clean sorted list — candidates can view their own standing too.

Deadline-Based Pipeline

Applications are active until the deadline. After it passes, the job moves to history automatically — no manual status management.

Instant Pre-Submission Score

Candidates see their match score before submitting — encouraging resume improvement and reducing low-quality applications.

Role-Based Access

Candidates and HR have separate dashboards — candidates cannot see other resumes, HR manages all jobs and results.
